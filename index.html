FAQ

Practical details, upfront.

A few quick answers before we start working together.

Do you register domains for us?

No. We do not auto-register domains. Our job is to research, shortlist and explain. You stay in full control of where and when you register.

Which TLDs do you support?

Domain Scout is optimised for .com and major European TLDs like .it and .ch. Other extensions can be added on request, depending on registry access.

How is pricing structured?

We work with a per-session flat fee, quoted in EUR, with clear limits on how many domains we scan and how many top candidates we deliver. No subscriptions, no surprise costs.

Is amamau available outside Europe?

Yes. While we are based in Europe and price in EUR, we work remotely with teams worldwide. Time zones and billing details can be adapted case by case.
