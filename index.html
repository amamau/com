How we work

Simple stack, tight scope, honest limits.

amamau is intentionally small. We’d rather have a few tools that feel serious than a catalogue nobody actually uses.

Every product starts from a real scene: a founder stuck on names, an agency that needs a shortlist, a small team trying to stop inbox chaos. We design for these moments — not for screenshots.

The output is always something concrete: a list of domains, a set of names, a structure for your inbox, a summary you can paste into a deck or send to an investor.

01 — Clarity before features

Each tool has a clear job and a list of things it will never do. That keeps performance, expectations and pricing under control.

02 — Explicit constraints

We prefer clear limits — how many domains, which TLDs, which exports — instead of hiding them in fine print or vague claims.

03 — Europe-first, usable anywhere

Pricing in EUR, privacy in mind and documentation that assumes a small team. The results, though, work wherever your market is.
