Phase • Private beta 01 — Domain research
Domain Scout

Long-form, curated wordlists generated from 10–15 seed keywords, designed to plug into Domain Scout and other tools.
  • AI-assisted expansion tuned for brand naming and domain search.

  • Exportable lists for offline work, scripts or third-party tools.
Ideal for studios that run frequent naming rounds. Join the early access list

How we work

Few products. Clear limits. Serious details.

amamau is intentionally small. We don’t try to do everything — we focus on the parts that make your company look precise and reliable from day zero.

Every tool starts from a concrete scenario: a founder in naming hell, an agency that needs a clean domain shortlist, a small team trying to stop email chaos. We design for these situations, not for slideware.

Outputs are built to be used in real work: decks, Notion pages, emails to investors and clients. No vague “insights”, just artifacts you can ship.

01 — Clarity before features

Each product has a sharp job to do and a list of things it will never do. This protects performance and simplicity, especially on smaller infrastructures.

02 — Explicit constraints

We prefer hard limits over marketing claims: max domains per run, supported TLDs, export formats, API usage. Expectations are set in advance, not in the fine print.

03 — Europe-first, global-compatible

Pricing in EUR, focus on EU privacy rules and documentation that assumes a small team. The outputs — domains, reports, structures — work anywhere you operate.
