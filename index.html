Domain Scout — The domain research tool built for real businesses.

We turn ideas into icons.

Domain Scout is a fast, registry-aware domain availability scanner for startups, makers and lean agencies. It runs bulk checks without API limits, works with custom wordlists and scores each available domain so you can focus only on names that work.

Focus: bulk domain search, smart filters, multi-TLD support (.com, .it, .ch) and a 0–100 marketing score for each free domain.

Why Domain Scout exists

Most domain checkers are built around third-party APIs, rate limits and monthly fees. That is fine for a quick single lookup, but not when you are exploring hundreds or thousands of options for a new product, brand or company name. Domain Scout is designed for that kind of work: offline-friendly logic, predictable performance and full control over your lists.

What Domain Scout does

At its core, Domain Scout combines DNS prefiltering with registry-level checks and a simple scoring system:

  • Bulk scanning of large wordlists or generated names, without per-request API costs.
  • Dedicated handlers for major TLDs like .com/.net (Verisign), .it and .ch.
  • Filters for domain length, prefixes, suffixes and blacklist words.
  • 0–100 score per free domain based on length, TLD value and basic readability.
  • Plain TXT and CSV exports, plus a Markdown report for teams and documentation.

Who it is for

Domain Scout is aimed at people who actually have to ship:

  • solo founders validating product and brand names;
  • small studios and agencies doing naming work for clients;
  • developers and makers who need a practical, script-friendly scanner.

FAQ

What is the fastest way to check domain availability?

For large lists, running a Domain Scout scan is usually faster and cheaper than calling a third-party API for every candidate. It filters with DNS first and then asks the registry only when needed.

Can I use my own wordlists?

Yes. Domain Scout is designed around wordlists and simple configuration. You can plug in your own lists, apply blacklist rules and generate brandable names on top.

Is there a hosted version?

Domain Scout can run locally or on a VPS. A hosted version and paid plans can be introduced later, keeping the same engine while adding automation and better UX.
