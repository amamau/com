Why Domain Scout exists

Domain Scout is an advanced domain availability tool built to give startups, makers and small businesses a real competitive edge in naming and branding. Traditional domain checkers rely on API quotas, high costs and rate limits — slowing down the entire research process. Domain Scout bypasses that by combining DNS prefiltering, registry-level lookups and dedicated handlers for major TLDs like .com/.net, .it and .ch.

What Domain Scout does for your business

Bulk scans without limits

Process thousands of candidates in a single run using DNS prefilter + WHOIS/DAS, with priority profiles (FAST, BALANCED, SAFE) depending on how deep you want to go.

Wordlists & brandable names

Feed your own wordlists, run dictionary mode or explore brandable and randomized combinations — with blacklist filters for words, prefixes and suffixes.

Multi-TLD, registry-aware

Different logic for different registries: Verisign for .com/.net, dedicated flows for .it and .ch, and flexible hooks to add more TLDs as your projects grow.

Marketing-first scoring

Each available domain gets a 0–100 score based on length, TLD value, blacklist rules and pronounceability — so you can focus only on the names that actually work.

Clean exports for teams

Export your results as TXT or CSV for quick sharing, plus a Markdown report that can be dropped straight into Notion, GitHub or your internal docs.

Built for lean workflows

Ideal for solo founders, small agencies and in-house teams that need serious domain research without adding yet another monthly SaaS bill.

Domain Scout — Frequently Asked Questions

What is the fastest way to check domain availability?

Domain Scout performs bulk scans without external API limits. It combines DNS prefiltering with registry-level lookups to keep scans both fast and reliable, even on large lists.

Can I search domains by wordlist?

Yes. You can provide your own wordlists, define blacklist rules, switch between dictionary mode and brandable mode, and even enable randomized exploration to discover unexpected names.

Is Domain Scout free to use?

The underlying engine can run without per-request API costs. Business-grade plans can later introduce automation, scheduled scans and dedicated support — while keeping the core logic accessible.
