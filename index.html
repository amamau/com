FAQ

Practical details, upfront.

A few quick answers before we start working together.

Do you register domains for us?
We do not auto-register domains. You remain fully in control. Our job is to research, shortlist and explain. You decide what to register and where.
Which TLDs do you support?
Domain Scout is optimised for .com and major European TLDs like .it and .ch. Other extensions can be added on request, depending on registry access.
How is pricing structured?
We work with a per-session flat fee, quoted in EUR, with clear limits on how many domains we scan and how many top candidates we deliver. No surprise costs.
Is amamau available outside Europe?
Yes. While we are based in Europe and price in EUR, we work remotely with teams worldwide. Time zones and currencies can be adapted on a case-by-case basis.
