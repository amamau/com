amamau in short

Design-driven tools for founders who care about details.

We created amamau because most tools for early-stage teams are either too generic, too expensive, or visually neglected. Our products sit in the middle: minimal, opinionated, and good-looking enough to feel reliable in front of clients, investors and partners.

Our focus is Europe-first, with transparent pricing in EUR and setups that work with standard infrastructure (shared hosting, basic VPS, popular registrars and mail providers).

How we work

Few products, strong boundaries, obsessive details.

We keep the roadmap intentionally narrow. Each tool has a clear scope, written limits and a simple story. That lets us care about typography, motion, microcopy and infrastructure without shipping a feature zoo.

01 — Design first, then features

Every product starts from the feeling: clean, trustworthy, quiet. Only after that we lock the feature set. If something doesn’t earn its place, we don’t add it — even if “everyone else has it”.

02 — Transparent constraints

Our tools are built to run on realistic setups: small VPS, shared hosting, regular registrars. We prefer stating hard limits (max domains per run, supported TLDs, export formats) instead of hiding them in the fine print.

03 — Europe-first, but global-ready

Pricing in EUR, focus on EU privacy rules, and documentation that doesn’t assume a huge team. But the outputs — domains, reports, structures — work globally.
