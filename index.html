Tools engineered for founders who care about execution.

We created amamau because most tools for early-stage teams are either too generic, too expensive, or visually neglected. Our products sit in the middle: lean, opinionated, and precise, so they look and feel solid in front of clients, investors and partners.

How we work

Few products, clear limits, obsessive details.

We keep the roadmap intentionally narrow. Each tool has a clear scope, written limits and a simple story. That lets us focus on clarity, performance and reliability instead of chasing feature lists.

01 — Clarity first, then features

Every product starts from a clear use case and a simple outcome: what it should do, and what it will never do. Only after that we lock the feature set. If something doesn’t earn its place, we don’t add it.

02 — Transparent constraints

Our tools are built to run on realistic setups: small VPS, shared hosting, regular registrars. We prefer stating hard limits (max domains per run, supported TLDs, export formats) instead of hiding them in the fine print.

03 — Europe-first, but global-ready

Pricing in EUR, focus on EU privacy rules, and documentation that doesn’t assume a huge team. But the outputs — domains, reports, structures — work globally.
