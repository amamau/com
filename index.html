Phase • Private beta 01 — Domain research
Domain Scout
Advanced domain discovery for .com, .it and .ch, built for founders who care about naming, not just availability.
  • Multi-TLD scanning with DNS + registry-level checks.
  • Brandability score (0–100) with filters for length, patterns and blacklist rules.
  • Resume-safe scans with progress tracking and structured exports (TXT / CSV).
Ideal for: brand studios, early-stage startups, solo founders. Request a beta run ↗
Phase • Design 02 — Brand layer
Brand Snapshot
A compact review of your domain, landing page and basic assets — built to answer one question: “Does this feel investable?”
  • Quick audit of name, domain and first-impression UX.
  • Clear pass / fix / drop recommendations in a single PDF.
  • Actionable checklist for the next 30–60 days.
Perfect before ads, investor calls or a rebrand. Join the waitlist ↗

Inbox Grid
A structured way to keep domains, mailboxes and aliases under control, before deliverability becomes a problem.
  • Blueprints for alias structure, role addresses and routing.
  • Simple, vendor-agnostic setup steps for popular providers.
  • Optional templates for autoresponders, privacy and support flows.
